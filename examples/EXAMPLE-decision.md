# Decision: digital notes instead of paper

**Date:** 2026-01-15 · **Decided by:** (name)

## Context
Paper notes got lost, weren't searchable and never with me.

## Decision
Everything worth keeping goes into the brain (~/Brain) — paper only as a
same-day buffer that gets captured digitally.

## Alternatives (rejected)
- Notes app without links (Apple Notes) — storage, not a knowledge network.
- Leaving things in chat histories — unsearchable, decays.
