# Spaced repetition beats marathon cramming

Reviewing with growing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 1 week …) anchors
material for good — an all-nighter lasts until the exam, not until real
mastery. Consequence for me: put important things into the brain early
and let it schedule reviews, instead of escalating before deadlines.
